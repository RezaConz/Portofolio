City Shield

CityShield is a project from the E-Goverment course. CityShield is an SOS application that helps users to immediately get help from the police when experiencing or seeing a criminal act with just one tap. This project focuses on the development of E-Government applications

App Features

Safe

Provide a sense of security for people

Forum

Forum that serves to share information with other people or the police

Editable Data

User can change their personal data

Fast

Providing assistance from the police quickly
